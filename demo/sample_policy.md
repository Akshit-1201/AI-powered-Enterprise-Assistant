## Leave & Time Off

- Full-time employees accrue **20 days of paid annual leave** per calendar year.
- Unused leave of up to **5 days** may be carried over into the following year; anything
  beyond that is forfeited on 31 December.
- **Sick leave** is separate and capped at 10 days per year; a doctor's note is required
  for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.
- Parental leave is **16 weeks** at full pay, available to all parents regardless of gender.

## Remote Work

- Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval.
- Fully remote arrangements require a signed remote-work agreement and a quarterly review.
- Core collaboration hours are **10:00–16:00 local time**; meetings should be scheduled
  within this window.

## VPN & Connectivity

- The corporate VPN (GlobalConnect) is required for accessing internal systems off-site.
- If the VPN **keeps disconnecting**, first try: (1) switch from Wi-Fi to a wired
  connection, (2) update the GlobalConnect client to the latest version, (3) clear the
  saved session and re-authenticate.
- Persistent VPN problems should be logged as a **support ticket** with the IT team,
  including the times of disconnection and any error codes.

## Expense Reimbursement

- Business expenses must be submitted within **30 days** of being incurred.
- Receipts are required for any single expense over **$25**.
- Reimbursements are paid out with the next payroll cycle after approval.
